Overview

CMS guide

  • Listing Card — controls thumbnail cards in attraction, stay, and restaurant listings.
  • Hero Image — the large public image on the detail page and listing card.
  • Detail Content — descriptive sections shown after clicking "View Details".
  • Booking Panel — the right-side call to action with price and partner link.
  • Gallery — additional visual slots on the public site.

Editor tips

  • Upload images directly — they go to Supabase Storage and return a public URL automatically.
  • Each form section maps to a visible front-end block, making editor training straightforward.
  • The Destinations tab manages country landing pages, including hero and planning sections.
  • Use the Settings tab to update the site logo and global configuration values.
